Good morning. Welcome to the community health information page. This browser extension helps readers understand web content in their preferred language.

English sample

Students can use this tool to read notices, scholarship information, and public service updates. The original text remains visible, so users can compare the source and the translation.

Please bring your identity card tomorrow. The registration desk opens at ten in the morning.

नेपाली नमूना

यो उपकरणले वेब पृष्ठको देखिने पाठलाई वाक्य अनुसार अनुवाद गर्छ। प्रयोगकर्ताले स्रोत भाषा र गन्तव्य भाषा सजिलै परिवर्तन गर्न सक्छन्।

कृपया सूचना ध्यानपूर्वक पढ्नुहोस्। आवश्यक परेमा चयन गरिएको वाक्य मात्र अनुवाद गर्न सकिन्छ।

Demo checklist

  • Choose Source and Destination in the popup.
  • Click Translate page in bilingual mode.
  • Highlight a sentence and click Translate selection.
  • Click Restore page before changing direction.
